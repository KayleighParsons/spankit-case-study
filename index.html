The project

Transforming an outdoor space with professional suspension

The challenge: A UK homeowner wanted to run string lighting across their garden between two fixed structures — achieving a perfectly level, taut catenary without specialist fixings, custom steelwork or a contractor.

Off-the-shelf garden fixing solutions couldn't span the distance reliably, required permanent fixings that risked structural damage, or simply lacked the load security needed for a permanent outdoor installation.

SPAN-KIT changed that. A single kit — supplied complete with KL150 locking devices and galvanised steel wire rope — gave the homeowner everything they needed to install a professional-grade catenary between two anchor points, with drops placed exactly where the lighting needed to fall.

No hot works. No specialist tools. No contractor. Just a confident DIYer, a wall to fence post span, and a result that's been performing without adjustment since the day it went in.

I never expected a professional wire rope system to be this straightforward. The kit had everything I needed — it's solid, it looks great, and the lights are exactly where I wanted them. — UK homeowner, anonymous submission

How it installs
1

Secure each end of the wire to a fixed anchor point using the supplied locking devices

2

Pass the wire through each Zip-Clip device in the direction of the arrow and thread through your suspension

3

Confirm engagement by pushing the pin in the opposite direction to the arrows — done
